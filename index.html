We are here to guide you to connect your TV



Set up with your remote


When your TV says, “Quickly set up your TV with your Android phone?” Use your remote to choose Skip.

Connect to Wi-Fi. Your system may need time to update.

Choose Sign in. Use your remote.


Prime Video:

Visiting the Amazon sign-in page on a computer or other device that can connect to the internet

Following the on-screen instructions after your device displays a 5-character registration code


Open the app on your TV:

Launch the streaming app or service you want to sign into (like Netflix, YouTube, Prime Video, Hulu, etc.) on your TV.


Select the option to sign in:

You should see an option to "Sign In" or "Login." Choose this option. Depending on the app, you may also see a prompt to "Sign in with a code."


Get the code:

Once you select the sign-in option, the app will display a code on your TV screen (usually a series of numbers or letters). This is the unique code you'll use to log in.


Go to the website:

On your computer, phone, or tablet, open a web browser and go to the sign-in page for the service. This is usually something like www.netflix.com/activate, www.youtube.com/activate, or the equivalent for the service you're using.


Enter the code:

On the website, you'll be prompted to enter the code you see on your TV screen. Enter it exactly as shown.


Sign in to your account:

After entering the code, you may be asked to sign in to your account by providing your username/email and password.

Once you're signed in, the app on your TV will automatically update, and you'll be logged in.


Enjoy your content!

After completing these steps, you should be able to start streaming content on your TV.

Troubleshooting Tips:

*Code Expired: If the code expires before you enter it, simply go back to the TV screen and you’ll see a new code.

*Incorrect Code: If the code doesn’t work, ensure you’ve entered it exactly as shown (case-sensitive and no extra spaces). Make sure you’re on the right activation page for the specific service.

*Multiple TVs: Some services allow you to sign in to more than one device (TV, tablet, etc.). However, there may be limits on the number of devices that can be logged in simultaneously, especially for premium services.
